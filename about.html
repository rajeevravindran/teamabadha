About Us

It all started when Prof D.S.S.Sudhakar with his vision of taking engineering coursework beyond classrooms alongwith a few students started the SAEINDIA Fr.CRCE Collegiate Club to develop and nurture the passion for automobiles among students in the college. With growing interest and number of members in the Club we decided to take a step further and participate in SAEINDIA - BAJAâ€™12. A team led by captain Ankush Nasre along with 24 other passionate engineers started to live a dream of building an ATV from ground up. The team cleared the virtuals round and qualified for the main event amidst the tough competition by 300 teams across INDIA.

Team Abadha has participated in SAEINDIA BAJA 2013 and 2014.Every year the team analyzing its previous performance has made significant improvement not only in the design of the All terrain vehicle the but also in fabrication ,marketing and management departments. This year we venture into a new field of designing , simulating, analyzing and fabricating a GO-KART. The team will be participating in International Series of Karting organized by Mean Metal Motors and International Go-kart championship organized by Lovely Professional University. The team is geared up for this new challenge to build the finest Go-kart.

Our Mission

  • Team Abadha is dedicated to designing, fabricating, testing, developing, racing, and promoting the finest vehicle.
  • We aim for better management and stepwise execution of our goals.
  • The team strives to provide a constructive learning environment for sharing of knowledge that will strengthen new team members by giving them the best hands-on engineering experience.
  • We commit to generate better relationship with industry sponsors.

With the expectations soaring high Team Abadha is all set to accomplish this feat through its dedication and teamwork .
